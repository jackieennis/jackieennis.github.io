Leaf

cloud-based patient records system

At Stanford's inaugural healthcare hackathon, Health++ (2016), my team and I built a prototype for Leaf, a universal, cloud-based patient records platform for iOS. By talking with the judges and other healthcare professionals, we learned about the nuances of healthcare innovation: regulation, privacy, adoptability. With more engineering skills under out belt, we're excited to return as upperclassmen and brainstorm new healthcare solutions.

Full documentation of the 24-hour project and our process can be found on Github and Devpost.
