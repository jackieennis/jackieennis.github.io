MINT Magazine

Stanford's premier fashion and culture magazine

My co-directors and I oversee the web presence of Stanford's premier student-run style and culture magazine. We started as a small, scrappy Squarespace gallery, and now manage a digital publication with thousands of unique readers from all over the world and a strong social media following. Along the way, we've learned a lot about content management, design, and digitizing a large organization. In fall of 2017, we were able to coordinate the launch of our brand new website with the launch of MINT's second fall issue.
